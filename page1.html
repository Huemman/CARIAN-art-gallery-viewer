Gateway Gallery

Gateway Gallery passed the 6-year mark in November 2020. Opened in November 2014, the art museum's 6-year milestone is truly worth documenting. Since its opening, it has been visited by close to 250,000 guests from all walks of life. It is undoubtedly still very young but already firmly rooted to its ground.
