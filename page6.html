Pintô Art Museum

Pintô International was founded in 2017 in New York City with a mission to promote the work of contemporary Filipino artists on an international stage. Created to support the mission of its parent institution — museum and non-profit foundation Pintô Art Museum in the Philippines — Pintô International is the New York-based entity that drives Pintô's global exhibitions and programming. The organization is committed to supporting the careers of pioneering contemporary artists of the region and to fostering global connoisseurship for their distinct artistic practices.
