Dramus Art Gallery

Dramus Art Gallery started in 1996 (Formerly known as Community Art Gallery and DTI registered as “Drawing Museum” Hence the abbreviation DRA-MUS), as an outreach arm for the NEO-TRIBALISM MOVEMENT or NEO-TRIBE (an Art Group founded by Leopoldo Aguilar and fellow fine arts students of University of the Philippine Cebu in 1993), to present Free Art Workshops and Mural Paintings in art agencies like NCCA, CCP, NIPPON PAINTS and LOCAL ART COUNCILS.
